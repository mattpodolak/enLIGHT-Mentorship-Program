Welcome

Welcome to the enLIGHT Mentorship Program website. By joining our community, your team will receive the opportunity to fuel your business by receiving valuable advice with regards to your startup. Our experienced mentors are looking forward to sharing their expertise to help your startups improve and succeed!

Mission

We aim to shine a LIGHT on youth innovation and ENtrepreneurship; to help youth discover their potential, pursue their passions, and contribute to the economic and societal growth of Canada through innovative ideas and businesses.

Our mentorship program will be the building blocks to guide student founders through connecting them with advisors with years of experience. Through working together, both the founders and the mentors will be able to benefit from their journey together! If you are willing to tackle challenges and overcome obstacles with the guidance of a mentor, sign up to be matched with an advisor today.
