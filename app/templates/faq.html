FAQ

What is enLIGHT?

We are a student-run nonprofit organization dedicated to youth innovation and entrepreneurship education, empowerment and support.

Do I have to own a seed or pre-seed business in order to qualify for mentorship?

All startups are welcome!

How are the mentoring matches made?

Team enLIGHT will be working towards matching all startups with the best match with regards to your startup’s needs.

How will I be communicating/meeting with my mentor?

That can be discussed with your mentor and is dependent on both parties’ availabilities. You will be able to communicate with the both online as well as in person, if this is their preference.

How can I be a successful entrepreneur?

In order to be able to be a successful entrepreneur, you must ensure that you continue to persevere and remain determined through both enjoyable as well as challenging moments whilst growing your business. Being the founder of a small business can be a difficult responsibility, yet through investing countless hours of hard work and energy, significant milestones can be met. Our team of amazing mentors will be able to ensure that you do not feel discouraged throughout this process, as they have once been in your shoes themselves!
